About


I’m Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google and Alphabet Inc. My journey in tech began in Madurai, India, where I developed a passion for innovation early on.I studied Metallurgical Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, then pursued an M.S. at Stanford and an MBA at Wharton. In 2004, I joined Google, leading projects like Chrome and Android before becoming CEO. My mission is to make technology accessible, drive innovation, and create products that empower billions worldwide.
